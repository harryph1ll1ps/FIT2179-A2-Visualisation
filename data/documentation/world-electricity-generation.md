# World nuclear electricity generation (monthly)

- **File:** `world-electricity-generation.csv`
- **Powers:** ridgeline + share line — *"Global Nuclear Generation (Monthly)"*
- **Source:** [Nuclear Energy Institute (NEI)](https://www.nei.org/resources/statistics)
- **Coverage:** January 2019 – March 2024, monthly.

## Columns
| Column | Description |
| --- | --- |
| `Year` | Calendar year. |
| `Month` | Month of the year (01–12). |
| `nuclear_generation_twh` | Nuclear electricity generated that month, in terawatt-hours (TWh). |
| `share_of_generation_pct` | Nuclear's share of total world electricity generation that month (%). |
