# Electricity mix by country (latest year)

- **File:** `electricity-mix-pct.csv`
- **Powers:** heatmap — *"How Different Countries Generate Their Electricity"*
- **Source:** [Our World in Data — Electricity Mix](https://ourworldindata.org/electricity-mix)
- **Citation:** Ember (2026); Energy Institute – Statistical Review of World Energy (2025) – with major processing by Our World in Data.
- **Coverage:** Selected countries, 2025, annual. Values are a percentage of total electricity generated.

## Columns
| Column | Description |
| --- | --- |
| `Entity` | Country name. |
| `Code` | ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 country code. |
| `Year` | Calendar year (2025). |
| `Fossil fuels` | Share of electricity from coal, oil and gas (%). |
| `Nuclear` | Share of electricity from nuclear power (%). |
| `Renewables` | Share of electricity from solar, wind, hydro, bioenergy, geothermal, wave and tidal (%). |
| `… (Original Year)` | Provenance columns: when a value was gap-filled with tolerance, these show the year the figure actually came from. |
