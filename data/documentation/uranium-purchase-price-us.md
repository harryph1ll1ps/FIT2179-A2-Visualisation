# Weighted-average price of uranium purchased by US nuclear reactors

- **File:** `uranium-purchase-price-us.csv`
- **Powers:** waterfall chart — *"The Price of Uranium — Boom, Bust, and Renaissance"*
- **Source:** [US Energy Information Administration (EIA) — Uranium Marketing Annual](https://www.eia.gov/nuclear/data.php)
- **Coverage:** 2002–2023, annual. Price is in US dollars per pound of U₃O₈.

## Columns
| Column | Description |
| --- | --- |
| `Delivery year` | Year in which the uranium was delivered. |
| `Total purchased` | Overall weighted-average price of uranium purchased from all sources ($/lb U₃O₈). |

## Notes
Reduced to the overall price column; the EIA source additionally breaks the price down by supplier, origin and contract type. In the original tables, a value of `W` means *withheld* for confidentiality.
