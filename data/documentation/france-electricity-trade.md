# France cross-border electricity trade

- **File:** `france-electricity-trade.csv`
- **Powers:** diverging bar chart — *"France Exports Far More Than It Imports"*
- **Source:** [RTE — Réseau de Transport d'Électricité (France), éCO2mix](https://www.rte-france.com/en/eco2mix/cross-border-electricity-trading)
- **Coverage:** Commercial electricity exchanges between France and its neighbours, 2025, in terawatt-hours (TWh).

## Columns
| Column | Description |
| --- | --- |
| `Country` | Neighbouring trading partner (or grouping). |
| `Direction` | `Exports` or `Imports`. |
| `TWh` | Energy traded in terawatt-hours. Positive = exports from France; negative = imports into France. |
