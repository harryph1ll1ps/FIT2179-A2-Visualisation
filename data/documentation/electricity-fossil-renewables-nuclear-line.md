# Share of world electricity generation: fossil fuels, nuclear, renewables

- **File:** `electricity-fossil-renewables-nuclear-line.csv`
- **Powers:** 100% stacked bar chart — *"How the world's electricity mix has shifted, 2000–2025"*
- **Source:** [Our World in Data](https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/electricity-fossil-renewables-nuclear-line)
- **Citation:** Ember (2026); Energy Institute – Statistical Review of World Energy (2025) – with major processing by Our World in Data.
- **Coverage:** World, 2000–2025, annual. Values are a percentage of total electricity generated.
- **Retrieved:** 28 May 2026 (filtered to the World entity).

## Columns
| Column | Description |
| --- | --- |
| `Entity` | Region — always `World` in this file. |
| `Code` | Our World in Data entity code (`OWID_WRL`). |
| `Year` | Calendar year (2000–2025). |
| `Nuclear` | Share of electricity from nuclear power (%). |
| `Fossil fuels` | Share of electricity from coal, oil and gas (%). |
| `Renewables` | Share of electricity from solar, wind, hydro, bioenergy, geothermal, wave and tidal (%). |
